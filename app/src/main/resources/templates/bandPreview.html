About the Band

The Lunar Shift is a dynamic alternative rock band known for their atmospheric soundscapes and powerful, emotive performances. Formed in 2018 in Seattle, the quartet quickly gained a dedicated following with their unique blend of post-rock guitars and soaring vocal melodies.

Their sophomore album, "Gravity's Edge," solidified their place in the modern rock scene, earning critical acclaim for its ambitious conceptual themes exploring space, isolation, and human connection.

Renowned for their high-energy live shows, The Lunar Shift continues to tour extensively, bringing their expansive sound to audiences around the world.
