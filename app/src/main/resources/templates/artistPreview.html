About

Elena Vox is a visionary electronic artist whose sound blends ethereal vocals with deep, pulsing synthwaves. Rising to prominence in the underground club scene, she quickly captured global attention with her breakout debut album 'Midnight Echoes'.

Her unique style draws heavy inspiration from 80s synth-pop and modern atmospheric techno, creating immersive sonic landscapes that resonate deeply with listeners worldwide. With a background in classical piano, she seamlessly bridges the gap between acoustic warmth and digital precision.

Currently touring across Europe, Elena continues to push the boundaries of electronic music production.
